After the IUT (DUT ou LP)

Here is the list of professions, classified by categories, accessible via a DUT in Computer Science and / or a Professional License in Computer Science.

Production and operation
Database administrator, systems and networks administrator, Operations analyst, Micro fleet manager, Help desk technician, Network and telecoms technician, Web designer, Webmaster.

Studies, development and integration
Project managers in IT services or ICT, Multimedia video game developer, Programmer Analyst, Project Director, Integrator, ERP Parameter.

Administration and management of the IT department
IT auditor, Director of information systems, Head of studies, Head of operations.

Support and technical assistance
Internal Affairs Officer, Trainer, System Analyst, Technical Director, Safety Manager, Safety Specialist, Method specialist, Network architect, Technical architect, Database architect.

Consulting in information system and project management (MOA)
Project manager MOA, Consulting - consultant in information system, Consultant in business intelligence, Security consultant, MOA consultant, ERP consultant, consultant specializing in not yet mature markets, Responsible for change management, Business IS Manager, Functional Project Manager, IS Architect, IT Correspondent.

Les poursuites d'etudes envisageables

The primary vocation of the IUT is to lead directly to the professional world, but it is possible to continue studying if the student really wants to.

After the DUT:
Engineering schools (ENSIMAG, ISIM, ENSEIHT, IIE, ENSSAT, INT, INSA, IFIPS, UTC, ...), Bachelor and Master of Computer Science, Bachelor and Master of Computer Science and Technology, IUP MIAGE (mastery of IT methods applied to management), Additional training abroad, Work-study training (preparation for DEST), Master in computer science, Professional license in computer systems and software.
